About us

Who We Are

Leading the Way in India's Mineral Industry

Founded in 1971, Kamlesh Minerals is a prominent player in the mining & processing industry, specializing in a wide range of minerals. From our base in Rajasthan, India, a hub of mineral wealth, we operate globally as manufacturers, suppliers, & exporters. Certified by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), Kamlesh Minerals maintains stringent quality standards, ensuring excellence in every aspect of our operations. Committed to innovation and sustainability, we strive to deliver reliable mineral solutions that meet global demands. Discover how Kamlesh Minerals can enhance your business with our comprehensive expertise in the minerals sector.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to establish a global benchmark in the mineral industry, renowned for our ethical business practices, unwavering commitment to quality, and sustainability. We are dedicated to creating lasting value for our customers by consistently exceeding their expectations and fostering a culture of continuous improvement and innovation.

OUR MISSION

At Kamlesh Minerals, we strive to lead the mineral industry with trust, offering high-quality products that meet diverse customer needs. Committed to innovation and operational excellence, we aim to make positive contributions to the environment, our communities, and industry development.

Meet Our Team

A Legacy of Excellence in Mineral Solutions

Founded in 1971 by the late Shri Lalit Kumar Ji Jaroli, Kamlesh Minerals began with a visionary mission to meet the evolving market demands of that time. He established firm foundations for the operational systems of the company, through which it has thrived and survived in the industry for over 50 years, setting benchmarks for excellence.

With the guidance of the late Shri Lalit Kumar Ji Jaroli, the company now stands as a strong generational entrepreneurial enterprise. Today, Kamlesh Minerals is led by Kamlesh Jaroli as Chairman and Bhavi Jaroli as CEO.

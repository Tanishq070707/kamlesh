KAMLESH ENGINEERS

Our Machinery Division

Udaipur is the capital for grinding minerals in India. There was always a need for quality grinding and micronizing machinery in the industry. As early as the 1980’s under the guidance of Mr. Lalit Jaroli, we manufactured world standard grinding machinery and equipment for making the dreams of the mineral industry of micronizing the minerals to the finest level. The demand for Indian Minerals from across the globe increased over the years for finer mesh material.

Considering the market demand KAMLESH Machinery division developed Pulverizers equipped with external Classifier and Ball Mills to give the finest level of micronizing. Today KAMLESH ENGINEERS is the largest supplier of modern Pulverizers in India. Almost all the Mineral Grinding units use various machinery manufactured by KAMLESH ENGINEERS which is almost maintenance-free and saves on costly downtime. Customers are also regularly appraised of new developments so that they are not deprived of modern technology.

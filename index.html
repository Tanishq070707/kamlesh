ABOUT KAMLESH MINERALS

Leader in Mineral Extraction & Processing

Established in 1971, Kamlesh Minerals is a distinguished group of companies specializing in mining, mineral processing, and machinery manufacturing on a global scale. With a steadfast commitment to quality and service, we proudly hold ISO certification, ensuring stringent adherence to international standards.

At Kamlesh Minerals, we prioritize operational excellence guided by ISO 9001-2015 standards to ensure a seamless supply chain and superior service delivery. Our dedication to innovation drives us to continually enhance our practices, leveraging advanced technologies for ongoing improvement.

Founded in 1971 by Mr. Lalit Jaroli in Rajasthan, a state renowned for its vast mineral reserves in India.
